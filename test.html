Getting together is the beginning; training together is the reward.

PRIVATE Training.

Individualized training driven directly to you.

PRIVATE Training is OutFit’s premium-level, concierge service. If you’re results-obsessed but time-challenged, our PRIVATE sessions are the answer to your prayers. These workouts are the pinnacle of convenience.

Read More

Pick your coach, your time, and your place — OutFit’s MTV (Mobile Training Vehicle) arrives at your driveway, curbside, or culdesac. Train 1:1 with your coach or bring along the “Plus One” of your choosing at no additional charge.

Remove the guesswork and stress of traveling to your exercise destination and even build your own PRIVATE Group with the family members, neighbors, and friends in your neighborhood. Mom’s groups and golf foursomes — this is the solution for you! And every PRIVATE Training session delivers a bespoke workout—built just for you—that’s customized to your goals.
