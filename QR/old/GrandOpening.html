What is OutFit?

OutFit is a mobile outdoor fitness training service launching in Fort Lauderdale metro and coming conveniently to a neighborhood near you! We provide amazing workout experiences with classes led by TRX certified coaches.

OutFit is the freedom of fitness. Subscriptions start at just $39.95 per month for unlimited access to any OutFit Open workout. Download the OutFit Training app to find amazing workouts near you and get 14 days of FREE.

Looking for more progression and personalized coaching? Our Team subscription unlocks access to the small group training that you'll find in our Team workouts. Experience the pinnacle of convenience and individualization at our Private Training subscription where your selected OIC (OutFitter-In-Charge) will bring the Van and workout to you!



What is a workout like?

OutFit Open workouts are designed to provide a fun, challenging, total body workout (mobility, stability, strength, power, cardio, core) individualizing it through intensity and progression over time. The primary benefit to your body is it is continually being challenged to adapt through increased movement and intensity. The outcome…IMPROVED caloric burn and physical changes of becoming a stronger, healthier, more fit you!
